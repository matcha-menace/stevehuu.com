Overview

HyperStars was developed as our Capstone project at NYU Game Center from Fall 2022 to Spring 2023, featuring a team of 8 and a few other contributors. The project was oversaw by professor Charles Pratt and announced at NYU's Summer Showcase at May 2023. Currently it has amassed more than 500 followers on twitter with more than 50k+ views.
